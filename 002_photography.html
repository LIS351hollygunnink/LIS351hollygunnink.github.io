Capitol, 2018, Photography

This is a photograph I took during my senior year of high school. This photo is the dome of Wisconsin's Capitol building. I used photoshop to edit and manipulate the original colors of the photo.

Culture Clash, 2019, Photomontage

This photomontage was created during my freshman year at UW for Art 107. I used photoshop to combine a vintage photograph with more recent images, such as a volleyball and a parasailor.
